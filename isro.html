ISRO

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is India's national space agency that is responsible for space exploration, research, and development. ISRO is a major part of the Department of Space (DoS) and is overseen by the Prime Minister of India. The Chairman of ISRO is also the chief executive of the DoS. Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is the space agency of India. The organisation is involved in science, engineering and technology to harvest the benefits of outer space for India and the mankind. ISRO is a major constituent of the Department of Space (DOS), Government of India.
